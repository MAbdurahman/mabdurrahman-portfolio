About Me

Hello, I am Mahdi Abdurrahman, a Software and Web Developer, living in the southeast region of the United States, working primarily with desktop applications, web applications, responsive website development, and databases.  I have a bachelor's in Criminal Justice, approximately three years towards a bachelor's in Computer Science, and I am self-taught in other information technologies.

I specialize in creating beautiful, reusable, and professional websites, using the best practices, standards, and guidelines.  This results in semantic and SEO friendly websites, which improves the quality and quantity of website traffic.  I put my heart and soul into my work!

When not writing code, I am continuing to investigate, experiment, and test to improve existing methods of software and web development.  For, I am not content to rely on past laurels.  Also, I have a strong interest in Mathematics and an appreciation for the Arts.  But, programming and computer science are my passions!

My Skills

soft skills

In programming and computer science, attention to detail is a vital skill. As a developer, one must be able to critically look at long lines of code and discern anything amiss. Having an eye for small details can affect a project's small elements, which in turn are impactful to the overall project. Being a stickler for details is an immense strength.

Although programming is the basic and central part of computer science, the ability to communicate effectively is a necessary skill, both verbal and written. When describing complex ideas in layman terms, advocating for a particular position, or comprising to modify a project, one must possess good intrapersonal skills. In short, one must be willing to actively listen, pay attention to verbal and nonverbal cues, and assert oneself in workplace communication.

Some people think computer science is all logic, reason, and proofs, but that is simply not the case. Creativity is a key skill to solving problems, and creating functional modern websites and web applications. When bringing many designs to life, one must be willing to think outside the box to solve problems and create unique solutions.

If there were ten commandments for programming and computer science, manage thy time wisely skill would have to be on the list. Because most jobs in the IT field have tight deadlines, competing priorities, and quick transitions between tasks. Establishing goals, organizing priorities, and minimizing procrastination are a few attributes to help one stay on top of various tasks and responsibilities.

Being a good team player is a great skill in any organization, but it is particularly important if one is working in computer science. Many times, software and web developers are expected to collaborate with a number of different groups within an organization to ensure timelines are met and expectations are fulfilled.
