About Me

Hello, I am Mahdi Abdurrahman, a Software and Web Developer, living in the southeast region of the United States, working primarily with desktop applications, web applications, responsive website development, and databases.  I have a bachelor's in Criminal Justice, approximately three years towards a bachelor's in Computer Science, and I am self-taught in other information technologies.

I specialize in creating beautiful, reusable, and professional websites, using the best practices, standards, and guidelines.  This results in semantic and SEO friendly websites, which improves the quality and quantity of website traffic.  I put my heart and soul into my work!

When not writing code, I am continuing to investigate, experiment, and test to improve existing methods of software and web development.  For, I am not content to rely on past laurels.  Also, I have a strong interest in Mathematics and an appreciation for the Arts.  But, programming and computer science are my passions!
